FAQ

Common questions

If you don’t see your question here, contact us and we’ll help.

What documents should I bring?

Bring a photo ID, W-2s, 1099s, last year’s return (if available), and any documents related to deductions or credits (education, childcare, mortgage interest, etc.).

Do you offer virtual appointments?

Yes. We offer virtual and in-person appointments.

How long does tax prep take?

It depends on complexity, but most returns can be completed quickly once we have all documents.

Can you help with an IRS letter or notice?

Yes. We can review your notice, explain what it means, and help you respond.

Still have questions?

Contact us and we’ll get back to you.
